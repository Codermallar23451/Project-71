DELHI PUBLIC SCHOOL (JOKA), SOUTH KOLKATA

:Delhi Public School (Joka),South Kolkata:

The DPS Society is a non-profit, non proprietary educational body widely recognised for its progressive approach and commitment to excellence. Distinction and diversity are the twin hallmarks of their philosophy and educational leadership and scholarly achievement, its mission. In the words of Salman Khurshid, the former President of DPS, the DPS schools are Schools of the 21st century. They aim to be the best in the world and are unique in contributing to the World Education Scenario. Founded in the year 1949 the DPS Society today runs one of the largest and most professional school chains in India and abroad. It has nearly 134 schools across the globe. Almost all the DPS Schools whether they be in India or abroad are affiliated to CBSE which is the largest educational board in the country. Thus Delhi Public School is a trusted name in quality education. Learning at this School is viewed as an exclusive experience, which is not only about gaining knowledge, but also about shaping character and life skills, involving a holistic approach to developing the kind of people our children will become.
